Create an account for HMCTS professional online services

Once you have an account for HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) online services, you'll be able to apply on behalf of clients for:

  • divorce
  • financial remedy
  • probate
  • civil money claims

You'll also be able to:

  • check the status of a case
  • update a case - for example to provide additional information
  • send messages to court staff
  • give your colleagues access to the case

Before you start

It will take around 5 minutes to create your account.

If your organisation has not used HMCTS online services before, we'll need to check your application. We'll contact you within 5 working days to confirm or to ask for more information.
